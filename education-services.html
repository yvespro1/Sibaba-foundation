The Villain

The Silent Crisis: Demystifying "Plastic Teeth" and Kimeo.

For generations, dangerous myths surrounding infant dental development have led to Infant Oral Mutilation (IOM). This includes the lethal practice of uvula cutting (kimeo) and the unnecessary, painful extraction of healthy baby teeth (falsely called "plastic teeth").

This is not just a medical issue; it is a human rights issue. Unsafe extraction methods lead to severe infections, massive blood loss, and tragically, the loss of children's lives. We are fighting to end these practices by bringing clarity and truth to communities.
