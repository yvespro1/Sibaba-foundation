Your Privacy Matters

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Sibaba Foundation ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to respecting and protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website or contact us.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our site:

  • Your name and email address when you submit our contact form.
  • Your message content as submitted through our contact form.
  • Technical data such as browser type and page visit (via basic server logs).

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and messages.
  • Send updates about our programs if you opt in.
  • Improve our website and services.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Contact Form & Third-Party Processing

Our contact form uses FormSubmit to route messages to our team. By submitting the form, your name, email, and message are processed by FormSubmit in accordance with their own privacy practices. We recommend reviewing FormSubmit's policy.

5. Cookies

We use only a small localStorage item to remember your dark mode preference. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal data you submitted by contacting us at sibabadentalclinic@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
